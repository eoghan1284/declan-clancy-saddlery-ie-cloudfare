In showjumping and eventing, the qualities of speed, endurance, obedience and athleticism in the horse have been admired throughout the world for centuries. All of Declan Clancy’s products have been designed to complement these attributes.

The finest leather is used throughout and is quality checked before any products leave the workshop. The craftsmanship that goes into each piece is second to none, along with excellent durability and strength.
